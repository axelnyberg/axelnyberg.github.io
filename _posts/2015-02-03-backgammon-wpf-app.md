A backgammon game for Windows created by me and a group of classmates at Jönköping University.

**Language:** C#<br>

**Features:**<br>
*Game music<br>
*Animations<br>
*How to play instructions<br>
*Two player mode<br>
*Pause and continue game<br>
*Mute game music

[Source](https://github.com/axelnyberg/Backgammon)
